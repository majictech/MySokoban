SOKOBAN


How To Play

The goal of the game is to push the boxes into sand .You are in control of the Player. The Player can move up, down, left and right by using the arrow keys on the keyboard. ... Walls surround a Sokoban puzzle and the Player cannot walk through them. Walls make up the layout of the puzzle. Boxes can be pushed by the Player.
